I'm building **AgentShroud** — an open-source, security-first transparent proxy for autonomous AI agents. The narrative is simple: AI agents are moving from generating text to taking real actions. The governance infrastructure for those actions doesn't exist yet. AgentShroud builds it — a proxy layer that sits between agents and the systems they interact with, enforcing policy and maintaining a complete audit trail.

Model-agnostic (Claude, Gemini, ChatGPT, Codex, local models). No SDK changes required. 33 active security modules, ≥94% test coverage. v0.9.0 nearly complete, v1.0.0 imminent.
